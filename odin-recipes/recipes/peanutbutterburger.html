Victor Ho's Favorite Foods

Recipe #2 - Peanut Butter Burger

Description

Second favorite burger from the same burger joint. Despite liking it as second favorite, I only started eating during the last year the burger joint was opened which was 2023.

It's an odd choice for sure but I enjoyed for what it was. It may not be as filling as the The "Hangover" Burger but it was an enjoyable burger to chow down. I've had it about twice though, so my impressions of it are summed up into two visits with the last visit being the last visit to the burger joint before it closed up.

Ingredient List

  • Double Meat! - Two 1/4 lbs, Patties
  • Crispy Bacon
  • Melted Chedder Cheese
  • Creamy Juf Peanut Butter

Instructions

Has no known instructions as it is a custom-made recipe by the business. And you know, business do not share their recipes.
