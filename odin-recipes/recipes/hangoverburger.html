Recipe #1 - The "Hangover" Burger

Description

One of my favorite burgers during the early parts of the 2020s, a personal favorite pick from a burger joint that has closed down since 2023.

Packed a huge punch when I first ate it back around 2021, it always filled up my belly. But within the last year of its running, I've had a hard time finishing up the burger.

Generally, 75% of it was finished up.

Ingredient List

  • 1/2 lb Fresh Ground Beef Patty
  • Deli Sliced Smoked Ham
  • Crispy Bacon
  • Tangy Meteor Sauce (or Ketchup!)

Instructions

Has no known instructions as it is a custom-made recipe by the business. And you know, business do not share their recipes.
