Recipe #3 - Bacon Cheese Fries

Description

This recipe's list of ingredients are here for show and best represents the food I like eating.

The cheese fries I'm thinking about is from a menu item at the Dallas location for Studio Movie Grill. When I picked up Moviegoing as a hobby back around 2022, the cheese fries at that location caught my eye. When it was first ordered, the experience was incredibly amazing.

It was hot, gooy, and it melted my toungue like a bottle of acid. It had the right amount of things to the point it became one of the first things I ordered during repeat visits to that location.

Ingredient List

  • 32 ounces frozen french fries
  • 1 tablespoon ranch seasoning
  • ½ cup freshly shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • C½ cup freshly shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ¼ cup crumbled cooked bacon
  • ¼ cup sliced green onion
  • 2 tablespoons ranch dressing

Instructions

Has no known instructions as it is a custom-made recipe by the business. And you know, business do not share their recipes.
